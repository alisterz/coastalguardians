Guardians of the Coast

We are the Whitecaps Coastal Guardians, Vancouver's premier supporter group dedicated to creating an electric atmosphere at BC Place and supporting our beloved Whitecaps FC through thick and thin.

From the stunning backdrop of the North Shore mountains to the passionate chants that echo through our beautiful city, we embody the spirit of Vancouver and the beautiful game.

Join us as we stand guard over our team, our city, and our passion for football. Together, we are stronger!

What We Bring

Thunderous Support

We create an intimidating atmosphere for visiting teams and inspire our Whitecaps to victory with our passionate chants and songs.

Community Spirit

More than just fans, we're a family. We organize community events, charity drives, and bring Vancouver together through football.

Visual Spectacle

Our tifos, banners, and coordinated displays make BC Place a sight to behold and show our unwavering dedication to the club.

Away Day Warriors

We travel near and far to support our team, bringing the Vancouver spirit to stadiums across MLS and beyond.

Original Chants

Our creative chants and songs reflect Vancouver's unique culture while celebrating our team's journey and achievements.

Year-Round Events

From season kickoff parties to off-season social gatherings, we keep the Whitecaps spirit alive all year long.
